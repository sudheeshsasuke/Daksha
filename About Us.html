Daksha

Daksha is a state level inter - colegiate cultural and technical fest of College of engineering Attingal , Trivandrum. Starting from 2005 , Daksha has been conducted annually , and each year its better than before. Daksha provided a platform for all college students to showcase their talents and also provides an arena to compete against the best in the country. Every edition of Daksha has a theme and we atempt to assimilate this into the very fabric of the fest.

Yantra

Yantra techfest is the annual science and technology festival of College of engineering Attingal. It also refers to the independent body of students who organize this event along with many other social initiatives and outreach programs round the year. Yantra techfest is known for hosting a variety of events that include competitions, exhibitions, lectures as well as workshops.
