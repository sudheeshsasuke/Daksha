CSGO 1v1

Details

Systems will be provided
Players may bring their own equipments like Mouse, Keyboard etc.
Registration will start at 8am and close at 4pm on event day
You can register through website also
Committee won’t be responsible for any failure like power, LAN, System etc.
If any, the current round will be restarted
Progress of previous round (Failed round) won’t be considered
Prize money for the winner will be Rs. 2000
No second or further prizes

RULES

  • Event consist of two rounds
  • They are Rifle round (map : aim_map_3G) and AWP round (map : aim_awp_row)
  • In case of tie after these two rounds, a tie breaker will be there
  • Tie breaker round will be a pistol round (map : aim_pistol_csgo)
  • Number of round will be selected by event committee
  • Match will start only when we got two players to play
  • If more than two players for participating, match will held by round wise
  • Only one player (Winner) should qualify for next round
  • Winners should present at the game point when next round begins
  • Timing of next round will be decided by event committee
  • Absence will be considered as disqualification
  • Final round will be held at evening
  • It will be announced only after closing the registration
