    PERSONA-DEBATE

    A team event that advocates or rejects a position posed by the resolution. Intended to be a clash of logic, analysis and evidence, prioritizing communication skills.

    RULES

  • Each institute will be represented by two debaters.
  • One will speak FOR and the other will be speaking AGAINST the given topic. After 5 minutes, the interchanging takes place( FOR will speak AGAINST and vice versa).
  • Repetition of points not allowed.
  • Medium of expression can be only in English.
  • Topic will be informed on the spot( will be related to current affairs) and 15 mintues preparation time will be provided.
  • Paper reading is not allowed.
  • Duration is 10 minutes.

    RANGOLI

    Rangoli is an expression of the creative self and is an expressive tool that can bring people together to work towards a common creative goal, transcending age, language and cultural barriers. This provides an oppurtunity for the new generation to to learn the art and thus keeps our tradition intact. Rangoli represents our rich heritage and is both an auspicious and a preliminary necessity in any religious ritual.

    RULES :

  • One entry per institution will be allowed.
  • Duration will not be more than 2.5 hours.
  • Participants should bring their own materials. Use of any reference such as greetings or printed material/ picture in miniature form/otherwise should be avoided.
  • The participants shall have to prepare the rangoli within the space provided by the organizers.
  • Rangoli should be made with rangoli powder only.
