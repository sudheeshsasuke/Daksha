About Us

CEAL

College of Engineering Attingal, established by Institute of Human Resources Development(IHRD), is a premier technical institute having well flourished environment for moulding professionals. College of Engineering Attingal, started functioning in the academic year 2004-2005 with two degree courses offered in Electronics and Communication Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering and later we got approval for Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

Daksha

Daksha is a state level inter - colegiate cultural and technical fest of College of engineering Attingal , Trivandrum. Starting from 2005 , Daksha has been conducted annually , and each year its better than before. Daksha provided a platform for all college students to showcase their talents and also provides an arena to compete against the best in the State. Every edition of Daksha has a theme and we attempt to assimilate this into the very fabric of the fest

Yanthra

Yantra techfest is the annual science and technology festival of College of engineering Attingal. It also refers to the independent body of students who organize this event along with many other social initiatives and outreach programs round the year.Daksha has been conducted annually , and each year its better than before. Yantra techfest is known for hosting a variety of events that include competitions, exhibitions, lectures as well as workshops.
